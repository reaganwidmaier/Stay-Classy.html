French Fries

There are many different styles of french fries. Some of my favorites are shoe string and waffle fries.

My favorite place to get shoe string french fries is McDonald's. They are salty, crispy, and overall delicious.

My favorite place to get waffle fries is Chick-Fil-A. This is also my favorite fast food restaurant and their fries is one of the many reasons that it's my favorite.
